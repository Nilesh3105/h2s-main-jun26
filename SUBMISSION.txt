Soft Reset — a private, research-grounded wellness companion for Indian students through board exams, entrance tests (NEET/JEE/CUET/CAT/GATE/UPSC), and result season. Check in on your mood in under 30 seconds, tag what's driving the stress, and get a matched, evidence-based micro-exercise — guided breathing, a thought-record, or behavioral activation. A private trends view shows mood over time, top stressors, and gentle insights, with a result-season mode around your exam dates. Verified India crisis helplines are always one tap away.

Deterministic-first: a pure, fully-tested rules engine and crisis classifier decide and protect; an optional GenAI voice (Gemini) personalises the wording, always with a deterministic fallback, so it runs correctly with no API key. Python/FastAPI/SQLite + React/Vite/TypeScript; accessibility (WCAG 2.2) and security enforced in CI; one-tap data export and delete. Private by default — no accounts, no third-party analytics. Not a medical device.
