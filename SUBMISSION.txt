Soft Reset — a private, research-grounded wellness companion for Indian students through board exams, entrance tests (NEET/JEE/CUET/CAT/GATE/UPSC), and result season. Check in on your mood in under 30 seconds, tag what's driving the stress, and get a matched, evidence-based micro-exercise — guided breathing, a thought-record, or behavioral activation. A private trends view shows mood over time, your top stressors, and gentle pattern insights, plus a supportive result-season mode around your exam and result dates. Verified India crisis helplines are always one tap away.

Built deterministic-first: a pure, fully-tested rules engine and crisis classifier decide and protect, so the app runs with no API key — and is architected to layer in an optional GenAI voice without depending on it. Python/FastAPI/SQLite backend, React/Vite/TypeScript frontend; accessibility (WCAG 2.2) and security enforced in CI. Private by default — no accounts, no third-party analytics on your mood data. Not a medical device.
